Getting Started with Planting

Planting crops is a rewarding process that requires careful planning and attention. Follow these steps to ensure a successful harvest:

  • Choose a sunny location with well-drained soil.
  • Dig holes approximately 6 inches apart for seed placement.
  • Place seeds in the holes and cover with soil, pressing gently.
  • Water daily to maintain moist soil, avoiding overwatering.
  • Monitor for pests and address issues promptly.

Tips for Healthy Growth

To maximize crop yield, consider these additional tips:

  • Use organic fertilizer to enrich the soil.
  • Rotate crops annually to prevent soil depletion.
  • Provide support for tall plants like tomatoes or beans.
