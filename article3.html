What is Crop Rotation?

Crop rotation involves changing the type of crop grown in a field each season. Benefits include:

  • Prevents soil nutrient depletion.
  • Reduces pest and disease buildup.
  • Improves soil structure.

How to Implement

Plan a 3-4 year cycle with legumes, grains, and cover crops.
