Types of Soil

Soil types affect crop growth differently. Here are the main types:

  • Sandy Soil: Drains quickly but holds few nutrients.
  • Clay Soil: Retains water but can compact easily.
  • Loamy Soil: Ideal balance for most crops.

Testing Your Soil

Use a pH meter to check soil acidity and adjust as needed.
